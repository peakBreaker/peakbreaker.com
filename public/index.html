About

Our world is driven by data

And I love the challenge of handling so much of it. I have built the devices which produces data, to productionizing machine leadning models on big datasets.

Handling data can get very complex, and traditional approaches to handling data breaks down when the data is too large. Your traditional developer team will most certainly fail when you wish to do analytics on traffic data over long periods of time.

Handling big data requires certain skillsets, and a good big data team should have good foundations in the following (in my opinion):

  • Programming Knowledge (Especially in Java, Scala and/or Python)
  • QA
  • Distributed systems
  • Big data tools (such as Hadoop, Spark, Kafka)
  • Visual and verbal communication
  • Databases & SQL
  • Basic ML (especially if the team is working with a Data Science applications)

I hope by looking at my portfolio I can prove proficiency in many of these areas. I'm not much of a Java guy though, but I do Python and Scala.
